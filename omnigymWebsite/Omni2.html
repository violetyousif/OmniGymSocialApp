About Omnigym

Omnigym was born from a shared vision among six passionate fitness enthusiasts and tech innovators. We recognized the need for a platform that not only helps individuals track their workouts but also fosters a true sense of community among gym-goers.

Frustrated with the lack of connection in traditional gym environments, we came together with a mission: to bridge the gap between fitness and technology, making every gym session more interactive, engaging, and motivating.

With Omnigym, we're creating more than just an app—we're building a movement. A place where gyms and their members can connect, track progress, challenge each other, and push toward their fitness goals together. This is just the beginning of a new era in fitness.

How It Works

Omnigym is your all-in-one gym community app, designed to connect gym-goers, track progress, and enhance your workout experience.

1. Partner Gyms Access

Omnigym partners with select gyms, granting exclusive access to members. Once your gym is onboarded, you can use the app to enter the facility, interact with other members, and track workouts.

2. Community Chat & Groups

Engage with fellow gym-goers through our community chat. Join different workout groups based on your fitness goals or interests. Share progress, ask for advice, and stay motivated.

3. Leaderboard & Challenges

Stay competitive with our real-time leaderboard. Earn points and climb the ranks based on your workout activity. Participate in gym-wide challenges and push your limits.

4. Task & Workout Tracking

Log your workouts directly in the app. Check off exercises as you complete them. Stay on top of your fitness goals with personalized task lists.

5. Seamless Gym Experience

Check in and access the gym through the app (where available). Stay updated with gym announcements, schedules, and events. Connect, compete, and grow with your gym community.
